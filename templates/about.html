About Me

Hi there! I'm Eli Nelson, or just Eli to most people. I'm a passionate individual with diverse interests including Minecraft, modding, coding, reading big books like Brandon Sanderson's *Way of Kings*, and playing Dungeons & Dragons (DND).

Though I'm still learning, I'm excited to grow as a Python developer. Coding HTML is okay, but let's just say I've got a love-hate relationship with CSS (trust me, you're not the only one!). I also enjoy advertising and sharing fun jokes with my friends.

What truly motivates me is the future – the possibility of seeing one of my websites used by more than 1000 people someday. It’s a challenge I’m ready to take on!

This site is where I hope to share a bit about myself and showcase my projects as I continue learning. Feel free to hang around and explore!
